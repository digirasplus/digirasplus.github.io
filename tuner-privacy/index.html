Guitar Tuner: Chromatic Bass — Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 10, 2026 · App: Guitar Tuner: Chromatic Bass (com.digiras.tuner) · Developer: Digiras
Short version: No accounts, no recording, no ads, no analytics. Your microphone audio is analyzed live on your device only — it is never recorded, stored, or sent anywhere. All settings stay on your phone.

1. Data we collect

None. The app has no server and no account system; we — Digiras — do not collect, store, or receive any personal information from you. The app sends no data over the internet.

2. Microphone audio

The app listens to your instrument through the microphone only while the tuner screen is active, to detect the pitch of the note you play. Audio is processed in real time, entirely on your device, and is never recorded, saved, or transmitted. No audio ever leaves your phone. When the app goes to the background, the microphone is released. If you deny the microphone permission, the app still works in reference-tone mode.

3. Data stored on your device

Your preferences (A4 calibration, instrument preset, language) and simple usage counters (e.g. how many tunes you completed) are kept only in local storage on your device. They never leave your phone and are deleted when you uninstall the app or clear its data.

4. Ads

This version of the app contains no ads and uses no advertising data, including the Advertising ID. If a future version adds optional rewarded ads, this page will be updated with a new effective date and the Google Play Data Safety declaration will be updated in the same release.

5. Permissions

  • Microphone — real-time, on-device pitch detection only. Never recorded or transmitted (see section 2).
  • Internet — a technical requirement of the app framework (Android WebView/Capacitor). The app itself works fully offline and sends no data.
  • Vibration — haptic feedback when a string reaches perfect tune.
  • Keep screen awake — prevents the screen from sleeping while you tune.

6. Children

The app is suitable for general audiences, collects no data from anyone, and contains no ads, purchases, or external links inside the app experience.

7. Changes to this policy

If we change this policy, we will update this page and its effective date. Material changes (such as introducing ads) will always ship together with an updated Google Play listing and Data Safety declaration.

8. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email tuner@digiras.com.
