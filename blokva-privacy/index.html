Blokva — Privacy Policy

Effective date: 4 July 2026 · App: Blokva (com.digiras.blokva) · Developer: Digiras
Short version: Blokva has no accounts, no sign-up, and no analytics. Your game data stays on your device. The only data that leaves your phone is through Google AdMob, which shows optional, reward-based ads and may use an advertising identifier to do so.

1. Data the app collects

The game itself collects nothing. Blokva has no servers and no account system, so we — Digiras — do not collect, store, or receive any personal information from you. The one exception is our advertising partner, Google AdMob, described in section 3.

2. Data stored on your device

Your scores, daily streak, progress (XP, rank, unlocked themes), daily missions, local leaderboard, and settings are saved only on your device using local storage. This data never leaves your phone, is never sent to us or anyone else, and is removed if you uninstall the app or clear its data.

3. Ads & advertising identifier

Blokva shows optional, reward-based (rewarded) ads through Google AdMob. These ads are never forced: there are no banners and no full-screen interstitials. An ad only plays when you choose to tap a "watch" button — for example to continue after a game, earn a streak freeze, or unlock a theme early. You can always skip these rewards and keep playing for free.

To serve these ads, Google AdMob may collect and use a device advertising identifier (Advertising ID) and related information, as described in Google's own policies. This is handled by Google, not by Digiras. You can learn more, and reset or limit your advertising ID, here:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • How Google uses advertising IDs · you can reset or delete your Advertising ID in your device's Settings → Privacy → Ads.

4. Permissions

Blokva requests the Internet permission (used by the app framework and to load ads) and the advertising ID permission (com.google.android.gms.permission.AD_ID) added by the Google Mobile Ads SDK. The game itself runs fully offline; the network is used only to load ads and never to send us any of your data.

5. Children

Blokva is a general-audience puzzle game and is not directed to children under 13. Because it shows ads that may use an advertising identifier, we do not target it at children. If you are a parent and believe a child has used the app, note that we collect nothing ourselves; you can reset or delete the device Advertising ID in the device settings described above.

6. Third parties

Blokva uses one third-party service: Google AdMob (Google Ireland Ltd. / Google LLC), for the optional rewarded ads described in section 3. Blokva uses no other analytics, attribution, or tracking services. Google's handling of data is governed by the Google Privacy Policy.

7. Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
