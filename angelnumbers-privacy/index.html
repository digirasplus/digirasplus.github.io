English

Thank you for using Angel Numbers (“the App”). We respect your privacy. This policy explains what data is processed and why.

Data we collect

  • Your name (optional). The name you enter during onboarding is stored only on your device (local storage). It is never sent to our servers and is not linked to your identity.
  • App preferences. Notification settings, your streak and your catch history are kept only on your device.
  • Advertising ID. The App shows rewarded (opt-in) ads via Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and share your device’s advertising identifier with Google for ad serving and measurement. See Google’s Privacy Policy.
  • Personal message generation (optional). If you unlock a personal message, the selected number and the name you entered may be sent transiently to an AI text provider to generate the reading. It is not stored permanently. Without this feature the App works fully offline.

Data we do NOT collect

No location, contacts, photos, files, health data, or payment information. No mandatory account creation.

Children

The App is not directed to children under 13.

Retention & deletion

Device-local data is removed by uninstalling the App. For any request, contact destek@digiras.com.

Purpose & disclaimer

Angel Numbers is for entertainment and personal reflection. It does not provide medical, financial, or legal advice.
